The Porche GT3

The GT3's body was based on the chassis of the Porsche 911 Carrera 4. The fuel tank capacity increased from 64 liters to 89 liters, and a tire repair kit replaced the spare wheel. Striking external features of the 996.1 GT3 included the front bumper with an additional plastic lip, the redesigned side skirts, and the double-decker rear wing , the angle of which was adjustable in six positions.
To improve airflow under the car, the 996.1 GT3 was equipped with a three-piece underbody made of polypropylene and featuring numerous ventilation openings.
The interior of the 996.1 GT3 was basic: Instead of the generously padded seats of the Carrera models, Recaro bucket seats were fitted as standard
